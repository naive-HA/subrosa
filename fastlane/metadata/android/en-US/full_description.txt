== sub rosa by naiveHA
Uncomplicated simple: "type" your long and complicated passwords securely stored in your favourite password manager on your Android phone with the touch of a finger. *sub rosa* is an Android app, built on top of YubiKit library provided by Yubico, which programs the static password of a YubiKey over USB or NFC. Once your done "typing" your long and complicated password, remember to wipe clean your yubikey... Is not much security if anyone can "type" your password by touching the yubikey!
